Privacy Policy

Effective: September 2025

Who We Are

Cal Coast Refrigeration ("Cal Coast," "we," "us") provides commercial and residential HVAC services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our websites, schedule service, or interact with our communications, including SMS text messages.

Information We Collect

  • Contact & Account Info: name, email, phone number, service address, company (if applicable).
  • Service Details: appointment preferences, job notes, equipment details, and related records.
  • Communications: messages and call notes, including SMS consent preferences (opt-ins/opt-outs).
  • Usage & Device Info: IP address, browser/user agent, pages visited, and referrers (via standard logs and cookies/analytics).

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve services, schedule visits, and complete work orders.
  • Send operational communications (e.g., confirmations, reminders, technician en-route notices).
  • With your separate consent, send occasional promotions.
  • Maintain safety, security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Keep records of SMS consent for audit and carrier compliance.

How We Share Information

  • Service Providers: vendors who process data for us (e.g., scheduling, messaging, analytics) under contracts that require confidentiality and appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal & Safety: to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to the protections in this Policy.

We do not sell your personal information as defined by applicable law.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site and understand usage. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent; some features may not function without them.

Your Choices

  • SMS: You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP. See our SMS Terms and SMS Opt‑In Policy.
  • Marketing Emails: use the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Access/Update: contact us to request access to or correction of your information.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records. SMS consent records are retained to satisfy carrier and audit requirements.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Region‑Specific Notices

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA (such as access, correction, or deletion). To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will verify your request consistent with applicable law.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy
