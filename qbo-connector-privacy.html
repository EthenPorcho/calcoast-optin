QBO Connector – Privacy Policy

Cal Coast Refrigeration (“CCR”)

Last updated: 2025-09-15

Overview

This policy describes how CCR processes data when using the internal QBO Connector that integrates QuickBooks Online with CCR’s ERP (Azure SQL, Power Platform). The Connector is for CCR’s internal business use only.

Data We Access

  • QBO accounting objects such as Customers, Invoices, Payments, and related metadata (e.g., balances, dates, document numbers).
  • Identifiers and contact details present in those records (names, emails, phone numbers, postal addresses).
  • Technical logs (timestamps, flow run IDs, error messages) for troubleshooting.

How We Use the Data

  • To sync customer and A/R information into CCR systems for dispatching, billing, and reporting.
  • To maintain accurate records and comply with accounting/audit requirements.
  • To improve reliability and security of the integration.

Sharing & Selling

CCR does not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing. Data may be processed by service providers (e.g., Microsoft Azure) under contract and only for CCR’s purposes.

Security

Access uses OAuth 2.0; transport is protected by TLS; data at rest resides in CCR’s Azure resources with role-based access, logging, and least-privilege controls.

Retention

Accounting data is retained according to CCR’s record-keeping policy and applicable law. Logs are kept only as long as necessary for operations and compliance.

Your Choices

Employees may request correction or removal of inaccurate internal entries through CCR IT. Customers can contact CCR to update contact information in QBO/ERP.

California (CPRA) & Other Rights

CCR honors applicable rights to know, delete, correct, and limit use of personal information, subject to legal exceptions. Requests: privacy@calcoastrefrigeration.com.

International Transfers

Systems are hosted in Microsoft Azure data centers in the United States.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date reflects the current version.
