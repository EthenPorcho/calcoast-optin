Cal Coast Refrigeration

This page explains how we obtain and manage consent for SMS/MMS messages (A2P), including how to opt in and opt out, in line with carrier, CTIA, and Twilio policies.
Live opt-in form (evidence for reviewers): https://ethenporcho.github.io/calcoast-optin/service-optin.html

Program Overview

We operate two messaging programs (collectively, the “Program”):

  • Cal Coast Operational Alerts (Non‑Marketing): appointment reminders, job/dispatch updates, service notifications, and urgent alerts for technicians, customers, or on‑site contacts.
  • Cal Coast Marketing Alerts (Optional): offers and promotions. These are sent only if you separately agree to receive marketing texts. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message types include SMS and MMS. All messages are sent from an application‑to‑person (A2P) sender.

How You Opt In

We only text you after you provide consent via one of the following methods:

  • Submitting your mobile number in a form that includes the required disclosures and checking an opt‑in box (unchecked by default).
  • Texting an opt‑in keyword (e.g., JOIN) to our number (we confirm with a welcome message).
  • Signing onboarding or service documents (including e‑sign) that contain the disclosures below.

Required Disclosures at Opt‑In

  • Program name and description (Operational or Marketing).
  • That message & data rates may apply.
  • How to get help: Reply HELP for help.
  • How to stop: Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Message frequency (e.g., “frequency varies by activity”).
  • Clear links to our Privacy Policy and SMS Terms.
  • For Marketing: a statement that consent is not a condition of purchase.
  • Age and ownership: you are the subscriber or customary user of the number provided and at least 18 years old.

First (Welcome) Message Disclosures

Your first message from us includes:

  • Program name and purpose (Cal Coast Operational Alerts or Cal Coast Marketing Alerts).
  • Msg&data rates may apply and frequency disclosure (e.g., frequency varies by activity).
  • Opt‑out and help instructions: Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.
  • Support contact information (see “Contact Us”).
  • Sender identification: “Cal Coast Refrigeration”.

Opt‑Out / STOP Commands

  • You can revoke consent anytime by replying STOP. We also honor common synonyms: STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT.
  • After you opt out, you may receive a one‑time confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re‑subscribe.
  • For help at any time, reply HELP (or INFO) for program info and support contacts.
  • If replies are not supported (one‑way notices), each message includes alternate opt‑out instructions (email or web form).

Message Frequency & Charges

  • Frequency varies based on activity (e.g., appointment changes or dispatch events).
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Recordkeeping

  • We maintain records of opt‑in/opt‑out (e.g., timestamp, source, consent language, and, for electronic forms, IP/user‑agent) for audit and compliance.
  • Technician consents are stored with HR records; customer consents are linked to the related service/job record.

Message Content Limits

  • We do not send illegal, harmful, or otherwise prohibited content.
  • Marketing messages require separate written consent and will clearly identify Cal Coast Refrigeration as the sender.

Quiet Hours (Marketing Only)

We avoid sending marketing messages before 8:00 a.m. or after 9:00 p.m. local time.

Privacy

Your mobile number and preferences are handled per our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your mobile number. We share it only with service providers needed to deliver messages, subject to contractual safeguards.
