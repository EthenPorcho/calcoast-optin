SMS Opt-In Policy

Cal Coast Refrigeration

Effective Date: August 2025

Purpose

Cal Coast Refrigeration (“the Company”) is committed to ensuring the privacy and preferences of our employees, customers, and contacts regarding SMS (text) communications. This policy describes how we obtain, use, and manage consent for SMS messaging in compliance with U.S. law and Twilio’s Acceptable Use Policy.

Consent and Opt-In

For Technicians (Internal)

  • Technicians provide their mobile phone number and agree to receive operational SMS messages (such as scheduling, job assignments, and urgent updates) as part of employment onboarding or HR forms.
  • Example language:
    By providing your mobile phone number, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Cal Coast Refrigeration regarding scheduling, job assignments, and urgent notifications. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

For Customers and On-Site Contacts (External)

  • SMS notifications are only sent to customers or on-site contacts who have explicitly opted in.
  • Consent is collected via digital forms, checkboxes, or written agreements that include clear disclosures:
    • What messages will be sent (e.g., appointment reminders, service updates)
    • Frequency of messages (if known)
    • Message and data rates may apply
    • How to opt out (reply STOP)
  • Example language:
    By checking this box, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Cal Coast Refrigeration about your scheduled service appointment or job status. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.

Opt-Out

  • Anyone receiving SMS messages from us may opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any message.
  • Opt-out requests are honored immediately and no further messages will be sent, except to confirm the opt-out.
  • Instructions for opting out are included in the first message to every recipient.

Recordkeeping

  • We maintain records of consent for all recipients (digital or written) for audit and compliance purposes.
  • Technician consent is stored with HR records; customer consent is linked to the customer account.

Message Content and Frequency

  • Messages are limited to information relevant to employment or services (no marketing or promotions unless separately consented).
  • Frequency of messages is disclosed where possible and will not exceed reasonable operational needs.

Compliance

  • We comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), and with Twilio’s Messaging Policy and Acceptable Use Policy.
  • This policy is reviewed regularly and updated as regulations or company practices change.
