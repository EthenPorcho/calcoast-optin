QBO Connector – About & Help

Cal Coast Refrigeration (CCR)

What this is

The CCR QBO Connector securely syncs QuickBooks Online data with our internal ERP (Azure SQL & Power Platform). It reads/writes accounting records to streamline dispatch, billing, and A/R visibility.

OAuth scope: com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting

How it works

  • Sign in to QBO via secure OAuth (Intuit).
  • Scheduled jobs pull changes (Customers, Invoices, Payments) into SQL.
  • When you create or edit a customer in the app, the connector mirrors it to QBO.
